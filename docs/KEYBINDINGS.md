------ | ------

<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>H</kbd> | Earlier page in history (page back)
<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>L</kbd> | Later page in history (page forward)
<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Alt</kbd> + <kbd>H</kbd> | Toggle notifications, except highlights
